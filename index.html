How many stars in the... gui

Here you can control the stars and text as they bounce around the interface.

What you can control:

  • Spikes: How many points the stars have
  • Wid: First radius length of star
  • Wid2: Second radius length of star
  • Text: Words on the screen
  • Speed: Speed of stars
  • Text Size: Font size
  • Text Color: Color of words
  • Star Color: Color of stars
